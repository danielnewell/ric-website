ABOUT US

We want to help you

Re-Imagine Communities is a not-for-profit 501(c)(3) organization located in Apopka, Florida. We are at the forefront of providing youth services to the disadvantaged and at-risk youth population of Central Florida. Our programs and activities are designed to be a catalyst to help members of the community reach their goals and fulfill their potential. Keep reading to learn more about the positive impact we have and join us in bringing about positive change.

Our Mission:


The mission of Re-imagine Communities is to advance equity by empowering the underserved, and at-risk youth of Central Florida. Through mentoring and coaching, education, life-skills training, job readiness training and financial literacy, we are committed to reshaping and redefining our communities one idea at a time.
Every day we work on planning events and finding resources for the community. We rely primarily on donations from the community to stay running. With contributions from people like you, we are able to help the community and expand our operations to reach more people in need throughout Central Florida.

Mentorship Program


In an ideal world, every child and teenager would be surrounded by strong and positive role models. However, in reality, that is often not the case. It has been proven that more too often, parents who struggle with poverty, substance addition, mental illness, domestic abuse, or lack of a support system for themselves, may not be able to be positive role models. Even in families that aren’t facing adversity, some youth may benefit from an adult outside of their family to look up to. This is where the Re-imagine Mentorship Program hopes to steps in and bridge the gap.

The purpose of our mentorship program is to expose students to positive role models. The program will work to provide avenues for students to access the professional individual in the community who will serve as mentors by giving them personal and career guidance. This program will help students:

  • Increased self-esteem and confidence when dealing with people and situations that take them outside of their comfort zones
  • Gain access to positive guidance
  • Build confidence to challenge oneself to achieve new goals and explore alternatives
  • Gain exposure to a professional network of people

This program is not only beneficial to the students but the mentors as well. Mentors will gain satisfaction of helping a student reach her academic and professional goals, receive recognition for service to the community, reinforce their own study skills and knowledge of subject(s), increase their confidence and motivation, and benefit from a sense of fulfillment or personal growth.

Re-Imagine Education


Re-Imagine’s Education Program is dedicated to advocating for minority, underserved, low wealth and at-risk students. The program is committed to finding creative ways to help students succeed. The program provides educators with the resources, skills and knowledge they need to create effective and creative learning strategies. Through collaborations, networking and pooling of resources, the program strives to create “out of the box” learning initiatives that will keep students, teachers and the community at large engaged. The overall goal of the program is to help bridge the socioeconomic opportunity gap that has been widened over the last decade.

Re-Imagine Life Program


Life skills are the competencies needed to succeed in life. Without them, young people are dramatically more susceptible to bullying, violence, substance abuse, dropping out of school, financial problems, incarcerations and unemployment.
Re-Imagine Life Program seeks to identify students’ needs and equip them with the resources and education needed to be successful. We believe that equipping students with the necessary life skills makes a difference. Students will learn problem solving, critical thinking, decision-making, creative thinking, interpersonal relationship skills, self-awareness building, empathy and coping skills. Learning these life skills helps children create a better future.

"We Ready" Job Readiness Program


Re-Imagine’s WE READY job readiness program focuses on preparing students with the information and resources needed to succeed when entering the job market. Students can apply the basic skills learned to any job at any level. Whether it's their first job or life long career choice, these skills will help students continue to succeed along their career path.
Our program explores

  • Career Exploration
  • Career Development
  • Professionalism
  • Financial Literacy
  • Leadership Skills
  • Goal Setting
  • Dressing for Success
We believe that if the students are “READY” they can conquer any obstacles and achieve their goals.
